**Definition (structure)**

: M is a structure iff

1. $|M|$ is the domain of $M$, which is a non-empty set;
2. For any name $a$, $a$ picks out $a^{M}$ in $M$
3. For any predicate $P$, $P^{M}$ such that $P^{M} \subseteq M$
