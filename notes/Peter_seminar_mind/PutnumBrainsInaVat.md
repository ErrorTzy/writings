**Example 1**: Crawling ants formed a picture of Churchill. Does this picture *depicts* Churchill?

> The *ants* surely does not depict Churchill, without doubt. We can distinguish the following three problems: 
> 
> 1. does the shape represent Churchill *for the ants*, and 
> 2. does the shape represent Churchill *for us*, and
> 3. does the shape represent Churchill, period.
> 
> According to Putnum, many people would say yes to (2), which I agree. But Putnum did not seem to distinguish between (1) and (3). According to Putnum, most people would say no to (3) because they would say no to (1), because the ants do not intend to depict anything. But it is unclear a. why the answer to (1) should determine the answer to (3).
